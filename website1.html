Our Story

A Black-Empowered Firm Built on Trust

Founded in 2013 by Mohmed Faisal Hassam, MFH Group was born from a vision: South Africa needed a professional, reliable, and competent black-empowered accounting firm that could compete at the highest level. Today, MFH Chartered Accountants Inc. serves clients across Gauteng and North West with a full suite of financial services.

Our mission is to provide cutting-edge, innovative, and dynamic solutions — ensuring every client, from sole proprietors to large corporates, receives timely, accurate, and value-adding financial guidance.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with small businesses and sole proprietors? +

Absolutely. We serve businesses of all sizes — from sole proprietors and startups to large corporations and government entities. Every client receives the same dedicated, professional attention.

What does B-BBEE verification cost? +

Fees depend on your turnover tier — EME (under R10m), QSE (R10m–R35m), or Generic (over R35m). Contact us for a no-obligation quote tailored to your entity size.

How do I register a new company through MFH? +

We handle the entire CIPC registration process, including name reservations, MOI filing, VAT and tax registrations, and UIF/PAYE setup. Simply contact us and we'll guide you through the requirements.

Are your services available outside Pretoria? +

Yes. MFH Group operates from both Gauteng (Pretoria/Laudium) and North West (Potchefstroom), and we can assist clients nationally via remote consultation and digital document exchange.

What are your office hours? +

We are open Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Urgent consultations can be arranged outside these hours by appointment.
