MFH Group is a black-empowered firm founded in 2013 with a clear mandate: provide professional, reliable accounting with no compromises.

Located in Laudium, Pretoria, and serving clients across Gauteng and North West, MFH Chartered Accountants Inc. delivers a full suite of financial services to SMEs, corporates, and government entities alike.

Founder Mohmed Faisal Hassam is an IRBA-accredited auditor who envisioned a firm that would combine technical excellence with genuine client care — a firm where every number matters.
