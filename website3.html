Our Story

A Firm Built on Integrity and Community

MFH Chartered Accountants Inc. was founded in 2013 by Mohmed Faisal Hassam with a singular vision: to build a professional, reliable, black-empowered accounting firm that serves businesses and individuals with distinction.

Today, MFH Group operates across Gauteng and North West, delivering assurance, taxation, advisory, and B-BBEE verification services to small, medium, and large businesses in both the private and public sectors.

Our values guide every client engagement: we believe that integrity, commitment, and genuine care for our clients' financial wellbeing are the foundations of lasting professional relationships.

Frequently Asked

Common Questions About Our Services

Do you service businesses of all sizes? +

Yes. MFH serves sole proprietors, startups, SMEs, and large corporates — as well as government entities. Every client receives tailored, professional service regardless of size.

How do you handle B-BBEE verification? +

We are accredited to conduct B-BBEE verification for EME, QSE, and Generic entities. We assess your scorecard across all elements and provide guidance on improvement opportunities before the formal verification.

Can MFH register my new company? +

Absolutely. We handle the full CIPC registration process — including name reservation, Memorandum of Incorporation filing, VAT registration, PAYE, UIF, and COIDA setup.

Do you offer remote services? +

Yes. While we are based in Laudium, Pretoria (with a presence in Potchefstroom), we assist clients nationally through secure digital document exchange and remote consultations.

What are your office hours? +

Our office is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Urgent after-hours consultations can be arranged by appointment.
