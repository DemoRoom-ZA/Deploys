What We Offer

Transport solutions built
for Southern Africa.

Whether you're travelling between cities or moving freight across borders, Tems Logistics delivers dependable, premium transport that keeps Southern Africa connected.

Passenger Transport

Scheduled and charter bus services connecting Johannesburg, Pretoria, Durban, Cape Town, and key Southern African destinations. Climate-controlled coaches with reclining seats, USB charging, and on-board amenities for a journey that arrives as good as it departs.

Cargo & Freight

Heavy haulage, general freight, and long-distance cargo transport using our fleet of purpose-built trailers. We handle everything from palletised goods to bulk loads — door-to-door or terminal-to-terminal, with full cargo documentation support for cross-border shipments.

Charter Hire

Dedicated vehicle hire for corporate events, school groups, government delegations, and private functions. Choose your route, set your schedule. Our professional drivers provide a premium, punctual experience whether you're moving 20 people or 60 across South Africa.

Licensed & Regulated

Fully licensed under the National Land Transport Act (NLTA) and registered with the Cross-Border Road Transport Agency (C-BRTA) for all Southern African cross-border operations.

Passenger & Cargo Insurance

Every journey is covered by comprehensive passenger liability and cargo insurance. You travel and ship with confidence, backed by full coverage from departure to destination.

GPS-Tracked, Always Accountable

Real-time GPS tracking on every vehicle means you can follow your loved ones or cargo at any point. Our operations team monitors all routes 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Professionally Trained Drivers

Our drivers hold PRDP certification, undergo regular wellness checks, and complete defensive driving training. They represent Tems Logistics' commitment to safety-first road culture.
