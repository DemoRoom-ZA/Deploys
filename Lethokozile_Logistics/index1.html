Our Story

Built on trust, carried across South Africa.

Lethokozile Logistics started with a simple idea: moving day shouldn't feel like a gamble. What began as a small courier operation in Johannesburg has grown into a nationwide network of trained crews, dedicated coordinators, and insured vehicles — all built around one promise: your goods arrive exactly as they left.

Today, we carry everything from urgent parcels to entire households and offices, treating each job with the same care whether it's a single box or a full relocation.
