Ekhaya means home. Every move we make brings you one step closer to yours.

Ekhaya Trading and Logistics was founded on a simple belief: moving day should feel like progress, not chaos. What started as a small courier operation has grown into a nationwide network of trained crews, dedicated coordinators, and fully insured vehicles.

Rooted in South Africa and built by South Africans, we understand what "home" means here — whether that's a family relocating across provinces or a business setting up its next chapter. Every parcel, every piece of furniture, every box carries that same promise: we bring it home, intact and on time.
